Hi, welcome to my website. I am a second year combined M.S./Ph.D. student at DSLab@POSTECH, under the supervision of Professor Wook-Shin Han.

Research Interests

My research interests are in Natural Language Processing, with a primary focus on Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).
I am particularly passionate about multimodal document RAG. My current work centers on enabling multihop retrieval from complex documents composed of text, tables, and images. In the future, I plan to extend this research to encompass a broader range of modalities and domains.

Previously, I focused on discovering schemas from JSON documents effectively and efficiently, which resulted in a first-author publication at VLDB.
